about me
I am a robot software engineer at Aldebaran Robotics. I build interactive applications for both the B2B and B2C markets.

Prior to that, I spent my time in academia as a researcher in both Human-Robot Interaction, at Tufts University, and Human-Computer Interaction, at Bowling Green State University.

Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with my family and hope to carve out more time for personal projects.

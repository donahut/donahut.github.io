about me
I'm Tom.  I am a robot software engineer at Aldebaran Robotics. I design and build immersive behavior and functionality into autonomous humanoid robots. I've worked on a wide range of projects across our stack -- from middleware modules to front-end web -- but my current focus is on core application development.

Prior to that, I spent my time in academia as a researcher in the Human-Robot Interaction Lab, at Tufts University, and Human-Computer Interaction, at Bowling Green State University.

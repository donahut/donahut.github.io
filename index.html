about me
I am a second year joint-Ph.D student in Computer Science and Cognitive Science at Tufts University advised by Matthias Scheutz in the Human Robot Interaction Lab.

My research interests include Adaptive Robot Autonomy, Multi-Robot/Human Teaming, and Distributed Metareasoning.

I thoroughly enjoy the process of learning and creating, whether in the classroom, the lab or at home.

about me
I am a robot software engineer at Aldebaran Robotics. I design and build immersive behavior and functionality into humanoid robots. Currently my focus is on generating dynamic natural language dialogs that yield intuitive, productive and meaningful human-robot interactions.

Prior to that, I spent my time in academia as a researcher in both Human-Robot Interaction, at Tufts University, and Human-Computer Interaction, at Bowling Green State University.

Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with my family and strive to carve out some time for personal projects.
